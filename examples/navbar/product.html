Hotline Miami

Hotline Miami is a high-octane action game overflowing with raw brutality, hard-boiled gunplay and skull crushing close combat. Set in an alternative 1989 Miami, you will assume the role of a mysterious antihero on a murderous rampage against the shady underworld at the behest of voices on your answering machine. Soon you'll find yourself struggling to get a grip of what is going on and why you are prone to these acts of violence. Hotline Miami's sequel/prequel, Hotline Miami 2: Wrong Number was released on the 10th of March 2015, it features 12 new playable characters as well as new weapons and levels and a level editor.
